## Part A: Refund & Return Policy

### A1. Eligibility for Returns

Customers may request a return within **7 calendar days** from the date of successful delivery. To be eligible, the following conditions must all be met:

1. The item is in its original, unused condition.
2. All original packaging, manuals, accessories, and warranty cards are included.
3. The return reason falls under one of the accepted categories listed in Section A2.

TechShop reserves the right to reject a return if the product shows signs of physical damage caused by the customer, tampering, or missing components.

### A2. Accepted Return Reasons

| Code | Reason                              |
|------|-------------------------------------|
| R01  | Product is defective upon arrival   |
| R02  | Product does not match the website description |
| R03  | Wrong product delivered             |
| R04  | Product damaged during shipping     |
| R05  | Customer changed mind (unopened items only) |

> **Note:** Reason code R05 (change of mind) is only accepted for items that are unopened and in factory-sealed packaging. A restocking fee of 50,000 VND applies for R05 returns.

### A3. Non-Returnable Items

The following products are strictly non-returnable and non-refundable:

- Digital products (software licenses, game codes, e-gift cards)
- Opened personal care items and hygiene products
- Opened in-ear headphones and earbuds
- Products explicitly marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable" on the product listing
- Products with broken seals where the seal-break policy is noted on the listing

### A4. Return Process

**Step 1 – Submit Request**
Log in to your account at www.techshop.vn, navigate to "My Orders", and select "Request Return" on the relevant order. Complete the return form and attach clear photos of the product and its condition.

**Step 2 – Review**
Our support team will review the request within **24 working hours** and notify you via email and SMS of the outcome.

**Step 3 – Return Shipment**
If approved, TechShop will provide a prepaid return shipping label for customers in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City. For other provinces, customers may be asked to ship via a designated carrier and will be reimbursed the shipping cost upon receipt.

**Step 4 – Inspection**
Upon receiving the returned item, TechShop will inspect it within **2 business days** to verify the stated condition.

**Step 5 – Refund or Exchange**
Following a successful inspection, the refund or exchange will be processed as described in Section A5.

### A5. Refund Timelines

| Payment Method        | Refund Method                  | Processing Time         |
|-----------------------|--------------------------------|-------------------------|
| VNPay QR              | Reversed to original source    | 1–3 business days       |
| MoMo / ZaloPay        | Reversed to original wallet    | 1–3 business days       |
| Credit/Debit Card     | Reversed to original card      | 5–10 business days      |
| Internet Banking      | Bank transfer                  | 3–5 business days       |
| Cash on Delivery (COD)| Bank transfer to customer account | 3–5 business days    |

> For COD refunds, customers must provide a valid bank account number during the return request. TechShop does not refund in cash.

### A6. Partial Refunds

A partial refund may be issued in the following circumstances:

- Only part of a bundle order is returned.
- An item is returned with missing accessories (refund amount reduced by the accessory value).
- A used/opened item is returned under special approval (a usage deduction of 10–30% may apply).

### A7. Product Exchange Policy

Customers may request an exchange instead of a refund when:

- The product is defective but a replacement is preferred
- The wrong item was delivered
- The customer wishes to exchange for a different variant (color/storage size), subject to stock availability

Exchange requests must be made within **7 calendar days** of delivery.

If the replacement item has a higher price, the customer must pay the difference.  
If it has a lower price, TechShop will refund the difference according to the original payment method.

Only one exchange is permitted per order item.

### A8. Manufacturer Warranty

Many products sold by TechShop include manufacturer warranties ranging from **6 to 24 months**, depending on the product category.

Warranty claims are separate from refund requests and may require direct inspection by an authorized service center.

Warranty does not cover:

- Accidental damage
- Water damage
- Unauthorized repairs
- Software modifications or rooting/jailbreaking
- Damage caused by misuse

Customers should retain proof of purchase for warranty claims.

### A9. Order Cancellation Policy

Customers may cancel an order before it has been shipped by accessing the "My Orders" section.

Orders marked as **Packed**, **Shipped**, or **Out for Delivery** may no longer be canceled and must follow the return process after delivery.

Refunds for canceled prepaid orders are typically processed within **1–3 business days**.

### A10. Lost or Delayed Shipments

TechShop is responsible for products until confirmed delivery.

Customers must report:

- Missing packages within **48 hours**
- Delivery discrepancies within **24 hours**

TechShop is not responsible for delays caused by:

- Incorrect shipping information provided by the customer
- Force majeure events
- Customs or government inspections
