## 1. Orders & Purchasing

**Q: How do I place an order?**
You can place an order through our website (www.techshop.vn) or mobile app. Add items to your cart, choose a delivery address, select a payment method, and confirm. You will receive an order confirmation email within 5 minutes.

**Q: Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?**
Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement, as long as they have not yet been packed. To cancel, go to "My Orders" in your account and click "Cancel Order". After 1 hour, please contact our hotline at 1800-9999.

**Q: How do I check my order status?**
Log in to your account and go to "My Orders". You will see real-time status updates: Confirmed → Packing → Shipped → Delivered. You can also provide your order ID to our support chatbot for instant updates.

**Q: I entered the wrong delivery address. What should I do?**
Contact our hotline immediately at 1800-9999 or email support@techshop.vn. If the order has not yet been handed to the carrier, we can update the address at no extra charge.

## 2. Payment

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
TechShop Vietnam accepts the following payment methods:
- Cash on Delivery (COD)
- VNPay QR
- Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, JCB)
- Internet Banking (all major Vietnamese banks)
- MoMo e-wallet
- ZaloPay e-wallet

**Q: My payment was deducted but the order was not confirmed. What happened?**
This can occur due to a temporary network issue. Please wait 15 minutes — the system will auto-confirm or auto-refund. If neither happens, contact us at support@techshop.vn with your transaction ID and we will resolve it within 24 hours.

**Q: Is it safe to save my card information on TechShop?**
Yes. TechShop Vietnam uses PCI-DSS compliant payment processing. We do not store raw card numbers — only tokenized references managed by our payment partner, VNPay.

**Q: Can I pay in installments?**
Yes. Installment payment (0% interest, 3–12 months) is available for orders above 3,000,000 VND when paying with supported credit cards from Vietcombank, Techcombank, or VPBank.

## 3. Shipping & Delivery

**Q: What are the shipping options and fees?**

| Shipping Type      | Estimated Delivery     | Fee                  |
|--------------------|------------------------|----------------------|
| Standard           | 3–5 business days      | 30,000 VND           |
| Express            | 1–2 business days      | 60,000 VND           |

**Q: Do you ship nationwide?**
Yes. TechShop ships to all 63 provinces and cities in Vietnam. Remote areas (islands, highland districts) may require an additional 1–2 business days.

**Q: Can I track my shipment?**
Yes. Once your order is shipped, you will receive an SMS and email with a tracking link. You can also track directly in the app under "My Orders → Track Package".

**Q: What happens if I miss the delivery?**
Our carrier will attempt delivery up to 3 times. If all attempts fail, the package is held at the nearest carrier depot for 5 days. After that, it is returned to TechShop and you will receive a full refund.

**Q: The item arrived broken. What should I do?**
Please submit a return request immediately with photos of the damaged item and packaging.

**Q: My package never came.**
If tracking shows delivered but you did not receive it, contact support within 48 hours.

**Q: I got charged twice.**
Duplicate charges are usually reversed automatically within 3 business days.

## 4. Returns & Refunds

**Q: What is TechShop's return policy?**
We accept returns within **7 days** of delivery for the following reasons:
- Product is defective or not functioning
- Product does not match the description on the website
- Wrong item was delivered

Items must be unused, in original packaging, with all accessories and tags included.

**Q: How do I initiate a return?**
Go to "My Orders", select the order, and click "Request Return". Fill in the reason and upload photos of the product. Our team will review your request within 24 hours and provide a prepaid return label if approved.

**Q: How long does a refund take?**
Once we receive the returned item and verify its condition:
- VNPay / E-wallets: 1–3 business days
- Credit/Debit cards: 5–10 business days (depending on your bank)
- COD orders: Bank transfer refund within 3–5 business days

**Q: Can I exchange instead of refund?**
Yes. During the return request, choose "Exchange" instead of "Refund". Exchange is available for the same product (different size/color) or a product of equal value.

**Q: What items are non-returnable?**
The following cannot be returned:
- Software licenses and digital downloads
- Opened headphones and earphones (for hygiene reasons)
- Items marked "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable" on the product page

**Q: I threw away the original box. Can I still return the item?**
Returns generally require original packaging. In some cases, TechShop may approve a partial refund with a packaging deduction fee.

**Q: My refund is late. What should I do?**
If your refund has not arrived after the stated processing window, contact support with your order ID and refund reference number.

**Q: Can someone else return the item for me?**
Yes, provided they bring the order number and proof of identity.

**Q: I used reward points—how are they refunded?**
Refunded reward points are credited back to your account within 24 hours after refund approval.

**Q: Can I return a bundle item separately?**
Yes, but bundle discounts may be recalculated and partial refunds adjusted accordingly.

## 6. Account & Loyalty Program

**Q: How does the TechShop Rewards program work?**
Every 10,000 VND spent earns 1 Reward Point. Points can be redeemed as follows:
- 100 points = 10,000 VND discount
- Points expire 12 months after they are earned
- Double points on all purchases during Flash Sale events

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email or phone number and you will receive a reset link within 2 minutes. If you do not receive it, check your spam folder or contact support.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
Send a deletion request to support@techshop.vn from your registered email. Account deletion is permanent and takes 7 business days to process. All personal data will be removed in compliance with Vietnam's Personal Data Protection Decree (Decree 13/2023/ND-CP).
